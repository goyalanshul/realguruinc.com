Common Questions

Got a Question? We Have an Answer.

Do you charge brokerage from the buyer?
For all Omaxe new bookings, we do not charge brokerage from the buyer. We are the authorized dealer, so our fee is paid by Omaxe. For resale deals, our fee is discussed openly before you commit — no hidden charges, ever.
Are these projects RERA registered? Is my money safe?
Yes. All projects — Omaxe Royal Nest, World Street, and Omaxe Chowk — are registered under Punjab RERA. The builder must deliver on time or pay you interest, and your advance is held in an escrow account, not spent freely.
Can I get a home loan for these properties?
Yes. Omaxe projects are approved by HDFC, SBI, and other major banks. We are an HDFC-approved Business Sourcing Associate and can help you get your loan approved quickly, at no extra cost to you.
I live abroad (NRI). Can I still buy through you?
Absolutely. Many of our clients are NRIs based in Canada, the UK, and the Gulf. We do video call walkthroughs, help with Power of Attorney if needed, and coordinate everything on the ground.
How does the 12% return at Omaxe Chowk work?
Omaxe Chowk is a mixed-use commercial project at Ghumar Mandi, Ludhiana — one of the city's busiest intersections with 50,000+ projected weekend footfall. HopUp units start at ₹92 Lakh and carry a 12% annual loyalty return during fitout. After delivery (June 2030 as per RERA), you earn lease rental. WhatsApp us for the current scheme circular.
What is the NH-152 residential project?
A premium high-rise residential launch on the Ambala-Chandigarh Expressway (NH-152), next to MM Hospital and Omaxe Greens. 3 BHK (1,800 sq ft) and 4 BHK (2,300 sq ft) apartments in an S+14 tower, Mivan construction. Pre-launch stage — RERA registration in progress. Call or WhatsApp for full details.
